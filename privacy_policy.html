Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Tic Tac Toe (the "App"), developed by Coralik. It explains how we handle information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App operates entirely on your device. No account registration is required, and no data is sent to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

3. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages. Because we collect no personal data from any user, the App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

If advertising is added to the App in the future, it will be restricted to users aged 13 and older in accordance with Google Play's Families Policy.

4. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no associated data security risk from using this App.
