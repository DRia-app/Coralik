FAQ

Everything to know before you play

How do bonuses and special mechanics work?
During a match you can trigger special powers: explode an opponent's mark to break their plan, freeze your move, or swap marks around. Keep the board's pace in mind too — after a few turns, marks can vanish on their own!
Can I play without an internet connection?
Yes, the game runs fully offline. Play it on the subway, on a flight, or out of town without any connection at all.
Do I need to pay to change the theme or unlock bonuses?
No — every bit of content in the game is free. There are no in-app purchases, hidden payments, or paid subscriptions; everything is available from the start.
If everything is free, how does the game make money?
Monetization is kept to a minimum and stays out of your way. The game shows a standard banner plus an optional rewarded ad that's used very rarely.
Is the game suitable for kids?
The game doesn't contain any inappropriate content, and you can easily switch sprites and backgrounds to cute, kid-friendly options. That said, the game does show ads, so some adult supervision is still a good idea.
Can I set up the game in a strict, minimal style?
Absolutely. If you'd rather focus on tactics or just want less color on screen, pick the strict interface elements to make the graphics clean and minimal.
