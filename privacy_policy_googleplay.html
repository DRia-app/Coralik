1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Tic Tac Toe app (the "App") handles information when you use it. The App is a fully offline game — it does not connect to our servers and we do not collect, store, or process any personal data ourselves.

The App displays ads served by third-party advertising networks. Those networks may collect certain data as described in Section 2 below.

2. Third-Party Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may also serve ads through third-party ad networks via mediation. These networks may collect:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • IP address
  • Device type, OS version, language, and time zone
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is used to serve relevant ads and is governed by each network's own privacy policy. Google AdMob's privacy policy is available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

A full list of ad networks that may be activated through AdMob mediation is available at: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/9012903.

3. Legal Basis for Data Processing (GDPR — EEA, UK, Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the legal basis for processing your data for personalized advertising is your explicit consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR).

On first launch, the App shows a consent dialog powered by Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) in accordance with the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework (TCF v2). You may choose to allow or decline personalized ads:

  • If you consent — personalized ads are shown based on your interests.
  • If you decline — non-personalized ads are shown instead. These still require a limited amount of technical data (IP address, device info) but are not based on your interest profile.

You may review or withdraw your consent at any time by tapping Ad Privacy Settings in the app's Settings screen. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing that took place before withdrawal.

4. Your Rights under GDPR

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the following rights regarding personal data processed by our advertising partners as independent data controllers:

  • Access — request a copy of the data held about you.
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Restriction — request that processing be limited in certain circumstances.

Because we do not store your data ourselves, most of these rights are exercised directly with Google. You can manage your data and ad preferences at: https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

5. Opting Out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time through your Android device settings:

  • Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID

After deleting your advertising ID, only non-personalized ads will be served. EEA/UK/Swiss users can also manage consent directly in the app (see Section 3).

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages for gameplay. However, personalized advertising is shown only to users aged 13 and older (or 16 and older where required by local law, including within the EEA and UK).

The App does not include age verification. We rely on Google Play's content rating system and Google AdMob's own signals to apply appropriate advertising restrictions. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at the address in Section 9 so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Retention

We do not collect or store personal data on our own servers. Retention periods for data collected by advertising networks are governed by their respective privacy policies. Google's data retention practices are described at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/retention.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We will notify users of material changes through the App or by updating the effective date on this page.
