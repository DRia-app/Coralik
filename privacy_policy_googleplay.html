Privacy Policy

App: Tic Tac Toe

Developer: Denis Riabov

Platform: Google Play

Effective date: June 15, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Tic Tac Toe app (the "App") handles information when you use it. The App is a fully offline game — it does not connect to any servers and the developer does not collect, store, or process any personal data.

The App displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may collect certain data as described in Section 2 below.

2. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. The following parties may collect, receive, or use end user data as a result:

  • Google LLC (AdMob) — primary ad network and SDK provider.
  • Third-party ad networks via AdMob mediation — the full list of networks that may receive data is available at: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/9012903.

These parties may collect:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • IP address
  • Device type, OS version, language, and time zone
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

IP-based ads personalization: Starting August 2026, Google is scheduled to begin rolling out IP-based ads measurement and personalization in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland (Google Vendor List Feature 3 — "Identify devices based on information transmitted automatically"). IP addresses may be used for ads measurement and personalization in addition to the Advertising ID. This is governed by the IAB TCF v2.2 consent framework — your consent choice in the app's consent dialog covers this purpose.

All data is collected and processed by Google and its partners — not by the developer. Google AdMob's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

3. Legal Basis for Data Processing (GDPR — EEA, UK, Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the legal basis for processing your data for personalized advertising is your explicit consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR).

On first launch, the App shows a consent dialog powered by Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) in accordance with the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework (TCF v2.2). You may choose to allow or decline personalized ads:

  • If you consent — personalized ads are shown based on your interests.
  • If you decline — non-personalized ads are shown instead. These still require a limited amount of technical data (IP address, device info) but are not based on your interest profile.

You may review or withdraw your consent at any time by tapping Ad Privacy Settings in the app's Settings screen. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing that took place before withdrawal.

4. Your Rights under GDPR

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the following rights regarding personal data processed by Google and its advertising partners:

  • Access — request a copy of the data held about you.
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Restriction — request that processing be limited in certain circumstances.

Since the developer does not store your data, these rights are exercised directly with Google at: https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

5. Opting Out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time through your Android device settings:

  • Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID

EEA/UK/Swiss users can also manage consent directly in the app via Settings → Ad Privacy Settings (see Section 3).

6. Children

This app is intended for users aged 13 and older. It is not directed at children under 13. The target audience is declared as 13+ in both Google Play Console and Google AdMob.

The developer does not collect any personal data from any user. All advertising-related data collection is handled exclusively by Google AdMob. Parents or guardians with concerns about advertising data can manage their child's ad settings via Google Family Link: https://families.google.com/familylink/.

7. Data Retention

The developer does not collect or store any personal data. Game scores and settings are stored locally on your device only and never transmitted anywhere.

Retention periods for data collected by AdMob — including Advertising ID, IP address, and IP-based personalization data described in Section 2 — are governed by Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/retention.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Users will be notified of material changes through the App or by the updated date on this page.
