Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Tic Tac Toe (Google Play version), developed by Coralik. It explains how we handle information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information ourselves. The App requires no account registration and sends no data to our servers.

However, the App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob (see Section 2), which may collect certain data automatically.

2. Third-Party Advertising — Google AdMob

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • Device type and operating system version

Google AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You may opt out of personalized advertising at any time via your device settings:
Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID
or visit: https://adssettings.google.com

3. Consent for EU / EEA Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the App will display a consent dialog on first launch, powered by Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP). This dialog allows you to choose whether personalized or non-personalized ads are shown to you.

Personalized ads are only served after you grant consent. If you decline, non-personalized ads will be shown instead.

To exercise your GDPR data rights (access, correction, deletion) regarding data held by Google AdMob, visit: https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy

4. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages; however, personalized advertising is shown only to users aged 13 and older, in accordance with Google Play's Families Policy and COPPA. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Data Storage

Game scores and settings are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
