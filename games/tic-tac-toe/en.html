Game features

Classic and extended modes

Use the familiar rules or add special actions that change the board.

3×3 and 4×4 boards

Choose a quick match or a larger board for longer play.

Special actions

Remove an opponent’s piece, swap pieces, or freeze a move.

Disappearing pieces

Track the move order and plan around the next piece that will vanish.

AI or local two-player

Play against the computer or together on the same device.

Offline and no purchases

All features are available immediately. The game works offline.

No ads between matches. The game only uses a small banner and occasional optional rewarded ads.

FAQ

Can I play without internet?

Yes. The game works fully offline.

Do themes or bonuses cost money?

No. All features are available from the start, with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

Can I customize the design?

Yes. You can change the background, board style, and the appearance of X and O pieces.
